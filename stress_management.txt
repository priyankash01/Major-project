Stress is a normal part of life, especially during work, exams, or personal challenges. However, it can be managed effectively with healthy habits.

Practice deep breathing or meditation to relax your mind.

Maintain a consistent sleep routine for better focus and recovery.

Engage in regular physical activities like walking, yoga, or stretching.

Eat nutritious meals and stay hydrated throughout the day.

Take short breaks to rest and recharge when feeling overwhelmed.

Talk to a trusted friend, family member, or counselor for support.

These habits can help lower stress levels, improve mental clarity, and promote emotional well-being.
